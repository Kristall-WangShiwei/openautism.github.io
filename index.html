Autism Health Dashboard

An OpenAutism Project

The Project

Autism Health Picture creates a beautiful and actionable visual representation of an Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) patient's health status, designed to increase awareness of the individual factors that can affect health.

Project Objective

Design open source "picture" (services and visualizations) of a patient with ASD that is accurate, timely, helpful, longitudinal, always-updating, and includes life data. This picture would keep patients and their care team up to date on their ever-changing health status, care plan evolution, needed behavior changes, and next steps to better manage overall health.
