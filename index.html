Autism Health Dashboard

An OpenAutism Project

The Project

Autism Health Picture creates a beautiful and actionable visual representation of an ASD patient's health status, designed to increase awareness of the individual's factors that can affect one's overall health

Project Objective

Design an open source “picture” (set of services + visualizations) of an ASD patient… that is accurate, timely, helpful, longitudinal, always-updating, includes life data, and informs patients + their care team of their ever-changing health status, care plan evolution, needed behavior changes, and next steps.
